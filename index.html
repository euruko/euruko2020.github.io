Location

The Savoy Theatre has over the years become an inseparable and vibrant element adding to Helsinki’s rich cultural scene. Savoy Theatre is particularly known as a leading concert venue for world music.

The theatre is located in the very heart of Helsinki, next to Esplanadi park and the major shopping and commercial streets. There are several hotels nearby and excellent public transportation connections.

Facts about Helsinki

Coffee

Did you know that Finns drink the most coffee per capita in the world? As a result, Helsinki has some great coffee shops and cafes…

Sauna

Nobody saunas like the Finns do. If you want to give this a whirl, there are several public saunas in Helsinki that will give you a warm welcome.

Oodi

If you have some spare time on your hands, you should go check out Oodi, Helsinki's new central library. It was recently chosen as the best new public library in the world.

Beers and drinks

Thanks to the recent craft beer boom, Helsinki has lots of bars with great beers. Finland is also home to the best gin in the world, Kyro distillery's Napue Gin.
