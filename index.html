Euruko 2020 postponed

Due to the huge amount of uncertainty caused by the current situation in Europe and across the world we've decided to postpone Euruko until May 2021. The new dates are 28.-29.5.2021.

We want to give special thanks to Shopify, Cookpad, Smartly.io, Woolman, and Taktsoft for sticking with us through this difficult decision and staying on as sponsors for the postponed event.

We are also grateful that our two keynote speakers, Matz and Linda, can stay onboard for the rescheduled conference.

Why?

The COVID-19 global crisis has made it very difficult to proceed with event planning for Euruko. We aren't comfortable with proceeding with the call for proposals and other planning until the situation normalises and it doesn't look like that is going to happen until at best 2-3 months from now.

At this stage we can still postpone with relatively little financial impact to the conference budget as we are still able to rebook our venue without any financial penalties.

Why not sooner?

Once we'd decided to postpone the event, the question that remained was for how long. While a shorter delay might be sufficient we want to avoid a scenario where we would be forced to move the conference dates for a second time. In the end 28.-29.5.2021 were the only suitable dates that were still available at our venue.

What happens to existing tickets?

All existing tickets will be automatically transferred to the new dates. If you are no longer able to attend due to the postponement, you can fill out this form for a refund.

Location

The Savoy Theatre has over the years become an inseparable and vibrant element adding to Helsinki’s rich cultural scene. Savoy Theatre is particularly known as a leading concert venue for world music.

The theatre is located in the very heart of Helsinki, next to Esplanadi park and the major shopping and commercial streets. There are several hotels nearby and excellent public transportation connections.

Facts about Helsinki

Coffee

Did you know that Finns drink the most coffee per capita in the world? As a result, Helsinki has some great coffee shops and cafes…

Sauna

Nobody saunas like the Finns do. If you want to give this a whirl, there are several public saunas in Helsinki that will give you a warm welcome.

Oodi

If you have some spare time on your hands, you should go check out Oodi, Helsinki's new central library. It was recently chosen as the best new public library in the world.

Beers and drinks

Thanks to the recent craft beer boom, Helsinki has lots of bars with great beers. Finland is also home to the best gin in the world, Kyro distillery's Napue Gin.
